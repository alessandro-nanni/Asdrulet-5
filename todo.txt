Items
    Weapons
    - Scythe: +13% damage for each dead ally
    - Torch: 10% chance to apply on fire to target for 2 turns


    Trinkets
    - Lucky Charm: 2% chance to deal 300% damage
    - Sattelite Dish: -10 stamina at the start of the turn, +6 defense
    - Twitching Talisman: 10% chance to follow up on an ability with another one (excluding ultimates)

    Chestplates
    - Leather Tunic: +4 defense
    - Mantle of the Usurper: If you have more health than the party leader, +5% damage and +7% max health


Classes
    Healer 60HP, 0 DEF, 80 stamina
    - Heal a single ally for 15HP. 45 STM
    - Single target attack for 8 DMG and apply golden touch for 2 turns. 50 STM
    - ULT: Clear all negative effects and heal all allies for 20HP

    Mage 70HP, 2 DEF, 90 stamina
    - Attack all enemies for 9 DMG. 60 STM
    - Apply Frozen to single enemy for 2 turns. 80 STM
    - ULT: +20 stamina for all allies, deal 20 DMG to a single target

    Beserker 80HP, 1 DEF, 60 stamina
    - Deal 12 DMG to a single target with a 20% chance to deal double damage. 30 STM
    - Apply strength to a single ally for 3 turns. 40 STM
    - ULT: perform 4 quick attacks on a single enemy for 9 DMG each with a 20% chance to deal double damage

    Paladin 90HP, 3 DEF, 50 stamina
    - Deal 4 DMG and apply taunt for 2 turns to a single enemy. 25 STM
    - Apply protection for 2 turns. 40 STM
    - ULT: Apply taunt to all enemies for 1 turn and gain thorns for 2 turns

Effects
    Golden Touch: when hit, attacker heals for 10% of damage dealt
    Frozen: Cannot perform their turn (make work for both players and CPU)
    Strength: +5% damage
    Taunt: can only attack the character that applied the taunt (area effects still have effect on other characters of that team)
    Thorns: 10% of received damage is dealt back to the attacker.
